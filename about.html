About the Developer


Benjamin Gallegos

Please note, this site is being used for educational purposes only. All images are accredited to their official hosts/artists as indicated on the page or in the footer.

Benjamin Gallegos is a current student at the Collin College for the Web Development and Software Development degree tracks. This website is the semester project final for the Interface Design class. This website was built to be responsive to different screen widths and devices as well as accessible through items such as alt text on images, easy to read font/typography, and keyboard focus.

This website was meant to shine a light on ocean pollution and the growing problem of plastics in our oceans. I chose this topic because it is meaningful and I intended to provide more information on the topics such as the Great Pacific Garbage Patch.
